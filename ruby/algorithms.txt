How to use the elevator:

- step into the elevator
- turn 180 to face the door
 - move over a foot to the right to face the button pannel besides the door
 - press number that equals desired floor number
 - press close door

 - If door opens and incorrect floor number
   - press close door and wait inside elevator

 - If door opens and desired floor number
   - exit elevator

How to make pasta:

- fill pot with water
- place pot on stove
- turn heat to high

- until water boils
  - watch stove

- add pasta to boiling water
- wait 8 minutes
- test a pasta for softness

- if pasta is hard
  - wait 4 minutes

- if pasta is cooked
  - remove pot from flame
  -strain pasta
